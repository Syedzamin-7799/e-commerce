VALORANT Points

VALORANT Points (LATAM/LAS/LAN/US) / Riot Point (LATAM/LAS/LAN/US) is a digital code that can be redeemed for Riot points which can be used for Valorant. Players compete with a global community of millions on fast-paced strategic battlefields in Riot’s games. Players can enhance their in-game experience by purchasing virtual items with Riot Points.

How to redeem the VALORANT Points or Riot Point?

  • Log in to Riot Game Client with your game account.
  • Click on the “Store” button which can find in-game.
  • Click on the “Purchase RP”.
  • Select the “Prepaid Cards” option and enter the digital code.
  • Select “Submit” to complete the redemption.
